Founder & Chairman – Dr. Althi Prudhvi

Dr. Althi Prudhvi, the Founder and Chairman of Varada Nexus Private Limited, is a visionary leader and medical professional with a deep commitment to service, innovation, and nation-building. A qualified general practitioner with a Fellowship in Internal Medicine from Apollo Hospitals in association with Medvarsity, Dr. Prudhvi brings real-world healthcare experience to the planning and execution of hospital infrastructure and consulting services. Beyond the medical domain, he has led the company’s expansion into multiple sectors including import-export, mining logistics, e-commerce, HR & PR consulting, and arbitrage services. His strategic vision and hands-on leadership have enabled Varada Nexus to become a trusted partner across industries. With a mission to make essential services more accessible, efficient, and ethical, Dr. Prudhvi continues to guide Varada Nexus toward sustainable growth and social impact.
